About

I'm a software engineer who enjoys building reliable software and documenting what I learn. This site collects practical notes from tools I use in everyday engineering.

The focus is on command-line AI coding agents. These tools integrate directly into terminal workflows and assist with code generation, refactoring, debugging, and exploration.

Every review on this site is based on hands-on use. I do not accept sponsorships, early access perks, or compensation for reviews. My goal is to provide honest, evidence-driven assessments that other engineers can use to make informed decisions.

If you have questions or feedback, feel free to open an issue on the repository.
