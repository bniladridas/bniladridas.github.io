Data & Privacy

Palmshed is designed to collect as little information as possible.

When browsing

  • No analytics.
  • No advertising.
  • No tracking cookies.
  • No third-party trackers.
  • No browsing history is collected.

When signing in

Signing in is optional. If you choose to sign in with GitHub, the website stores a small amount of information in your browser so your session can be remembered:

  • display name
  • GitHub username
  • avatar
  • authentication provider

This information remains on your device until you sign out or clear your browser storage.

Temporary data is also used during sign-in to protect against request forgery and to return you to the page you were viewing.

No server-side session is maintained.

Philosophy

Collect only what is necessary. Store only what belongs to the visitor. Track nothing.
